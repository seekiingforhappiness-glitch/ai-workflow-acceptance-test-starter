FAQ

What exactly do I receive each week?

One PDF (about 3 pages): the 8 fixed KPI slots with week-over-week changes, anomaly flags for any movement beyond ±30% with a plain-English interpretation, and three suggested next actions. A Markdown/Notion version is available on request.

Where does my data come from?

From you: CSV exports of GA4, Stripe, Shopify or similar tools, uploaded weekly, or read-only access you grant. During presale there is no OAuth app and nothing to install. We store the minimum needed and delete on request.

When does payment actually start?

When 3 intents are collected across the presale and trial plans, we email everyone on the list a checkout link. Nothing is charged before that, and reserving an intent does not obligate you to pay when the link arrives.

How does the $49 trial relate to the subscription?

The trial is a single one-time report — a low-risk way to see the exact deliverable. After delivery (and your one revision round, if used), we’ll offer the subscription. The trial is not a subscription and does not auto-renew.

What happens if my data is late or incomplete?

The report has a freshness gate: if data isn’t in by the day before an issue (T-1), that issue is delayed and labeled as such. Missing fields are marked “no data”. We never pad or invent numbers.

Why $129 per month?

Self-serve dashboard tools in this space list at roughly $59–$799/mo (Databox $159–799/mo, AgencyAnalytics $59–179/mo, from their public pricing pages, checked 2026-07-22) — and those still require you to build and read the dashboards. This service is done-for-you. Note: $129/mo is provisional launch pricing (assumed), pending validation by actual paid orders.

Can I cancel?

The subscription can be canceled anytime, effective at the end of the current billing cycle. The trial is one-time. The refund policy is not final until checkout opens and will be published with it — no refund promise is made on this presale page.

Who is behind this?

An independent builder validating demand before full automation is built — that is why this is a presale with an intent gate rather than an open checkout. Early customers receive the founding anchor price and direct input on the report format.
