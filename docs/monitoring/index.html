FAQ

How does the 7-day free trial work?

You submit the form with three public URLs. We confirm setup by email, monitor those pages daily for seven days, and send you one full weekly report at the end of the week. No card is required and nothing renews automatically — if you do nothing, the trial simply ends.

Can the weekly report be in Chinese?

Yes. Every report can be delivered in English or Chinese (中文) — your choice in the form, and you can switch language between report cycles by replying to any report email.

What exactly counts as a “public page”?

Any page that opens in a normal browser without logging in: pricing pages, product pages, blogs, changelogs, careers pages, public terms and policy pages, official news pages. If a page requires an account, a paywall or a CAPTCHA, it is out of scope — we will not monitor it.

Do you monitor social media or pages behind a login?

No. We never access login-required content, private groups or personal data, and we never bypass access controls. Checks are rate-limited and follow each site’s robots.txt and terms of service.

Can I change my URLs or cancel anytime?

Yes. Reply to any report email to swap monitored pages before the next cycle. You can cancel a paid plan at any time and it takes effect at the next billing cycle — no cancellation fees, no minimum term.
